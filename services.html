Typical deployment flow

A clear, repeatable process helps teams know what to expect at each stage.

1. Requirement capture Understand clinical use-cases, locations, and staffing patterns.
2. Device configuration Prepare recommended device models, accessories, and integration notes.
3. Installation & testing Install devices at sites, complete checks, and sign-off with teams.
4. Training & handover Train key users, share quick-reference guides, and assign support contacts.
5. Ongoing maintenance Preventive checks, replacements, and performance reviews.

Service questions we often hear

Here are a few simple answers—when you contact us, we tailor the details to your setup and locations.

How fast can you install?

For smaller setups, installation and training can often be planned within a few working days from confirmation.

Do you support multi-site rollouts?

Yes. We help you phase rollouts and standardize documentation so each facility knows what to expect.

What about staff changes?

Refresher training sessions can be planned so new staff understand how to use and care for the devices.
